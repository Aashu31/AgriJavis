Optimal Cereal Cultivation

Get AI-powered recommendations for wheat, rice, maize, and other cereal crops based on your soil conditions and climate.

Rice

Best grown in clayey soil with standing water. Requires temperatures between 20-35°C. Major growing states: West Bengal, Punjab, Uttar Pradesh.

Wheat

Thrives in well-drained loamy soil. Ideal temperature range 10-25°C. Major growing states: Uttar Pradesh, Punjab, Haryana.

Maize

Grows well in sandy loam to clay loam soils. Requires 18-27°C temperature. Major growing states: Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh, Bihar.

Pulse Cultivation Guide

Essential protein sources that improve soil fertility through nitrogen fixation.

Chickpea (Chana)

Grows in well-drained sandy loam soils. Temperature range 15-30°C. Major growing states: Madhya Pradesh, Rajasthan, Maharashtra.

Pigeon Pea (Arhar)

Drought-resistant, grows in various soils. Ideal temperature 20-30°C. Major growing states: Maharashtra, Karnataka, Madhya Pradesh.

Lentil (Masoor)

Prefers well-drained loamy soils. Temperature range 15-25°C. Major growing states: Uttar Pradesh, Madhya Pradesh, Bihar.

Vegetable Farming

High-value crops with short growing seasons and good market demand.

Tomato

Requires well-drained sandy loam soil. Temperature range 15-30°C. Major growing states: Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka, Odisha.

Potato

Grows best in loose, well-drained soil. Temperature range 15-20°C. Major growing states: Uttar Pradesh, West Bengal, Bihar.

Onion

Prefers light loamy soil. Temperature range 13-24°C. Major growing states: Maharashtra, Karnataka, Gujarat.

Fruit Orchards

Perennial crops with high economic value and nutritional benefits.

Mango

Grows in well-drained soils. Temperature range 24-27°C. Major growing states: Uttar Pradesh, Andhra Pradesh, Karnataka.

Banana

Requires rich, well-drained soil. Temperature range 15-35°C. Major growing states: Maharashtra, Tamil Nadu, Gujarat.

Apple

Needs well-drained loamy soils. Temperature range 21-24°C. Major growing states: Jammu & Kashmir, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand.

Cash Crops

High-value commercial crops with significant export potential.

Sugarcane

Grows in medium to heavy soils. Temperature range 20-30°C. Major growing states: Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, Karnataka.

Cotton

Prefers black cotton soil. Temperature range 21-30°C. Major growing states: Gujarat, Maharashtra, Telangana.

Tea

Requires well-drained acidic soils. Temperature range 13-30°C. Major growing states: Assam, West Bengal, Tamil Nadu.
